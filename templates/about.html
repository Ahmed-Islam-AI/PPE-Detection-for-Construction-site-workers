About SafetyGuard

Our Mission

Enhancing construction site safety through AI-powered monitoring and real-time compliance tracking.

SafetyGuard is an innovative safety monitoring system designed specifically for construction sites. Our system uses advanced computer vision and artificial intelligence to ensure that all workers are properly equipped with required safety gear and following safety protocols.

Key Features

  • Real-time safety gear detection
  • Automated employee attendance tracking
  • Instant violation alerts
  • Comprehensive analytics and reporting
Safety First

Ensuring all workers wear proper safety gear including helmets, vests, gloves, and safety boots.

24/7 Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of construction sites to maintain safety standards at all times.

Data-Driven Insights

Detailed analytics and reports to help improve safety measures and compliance rates.
